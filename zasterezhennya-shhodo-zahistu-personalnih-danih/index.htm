Notice regarding the protection of personal data general information

Personal data is data that can be used to identify you personally.

Data collection on this website.

Who is responsible for data collection on this website?

Data processing on this website is carried out by the website `s operator.

How do we collect your data?

Your data is collected when you provide it to us. Other data is collected automatically or with your consent by our IT systems when you visit the website. First of all this is technical data (for example, Internet browser, operating system or page view time). This data is collected automatically when you visit this site.

Why do we use your data?

Some of the data is collected in order to provide the website without errors. Other data may be used to analyze user behavior and actions.

What rights do you have regarding your data?

You have the right to receive information about the origin, recipient and purpose of your stored personal data free of charge at any time. You also have the right to request the correction or deletion of this data. If you have consented to data processing, you may withdraw this consent at any time in the future. You also have the right to request the restriction of the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances. You also have the right to file a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

You can contact us at any time at the address provided in the contact details if you have further questions about data protection.

Legislation regulating the processing of personal data

The processing of your personal data takes place in accordance with the requirements of the Law of Ukraine «On the Protection of Personal Data». The processing of personal data of customers located in the EU is regulated, in particular, by the EU General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (hereinafter – «GDPR»).

If you would like to know which of your personal data we process, you can send a request for this information at any time. You can find the list of data that we must provide to you in Articles 13 and 14 of the GDPR.

At the same time during the application you must inform us of your specific requirements so that we can legally consider your request and provide an answer.

The right to correct data about you

If you find that some of the personal data we are processing about you is incorrect or out of date, please let us know. In this case we may ask you to provide an identity document.

Withdrawal of consent to the processing of personal data and the right to deletion

Processing can be stopped at any time. It is enough to withdraw such processing.

You can also exercise your right to have information about you deleted. In the cases provided for in Article 17 of the GDPR we will delete your personal data that we process, except for those personal data that we are obliged to retain in accordance with the requirements of the law.
