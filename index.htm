The Idea

Education

— as a key to Ukraine’s new tomorrow.

Every Ukrainian kid must have a chance to remain a Ukrainian student.

The Challange

You can help us provide students in Ukraine with overall safety, constant internet connection and learning process integrity.

The governmental educational system

is unable to withstand the pressure of wartime conditions.

While private education can ensure students the proper continuity of educational processes and a good learning environment.

The Project

Claris Verbis Scholarship Project has operated sustainably through the years.

Despite the war, we continue searching for more children who need support in receiving a high-quality private education. 

Our partnership can open new perspectives for more Ukrainian families in the challenging circumstances they face.

    Popular questions

    Our new reality is unstable and vulnerable, yet we are brave enough to accept it and try to explain the way it works to you.

    infoeducarepay@gmail.com Please feel free to contact us with any additional questions

    What is the annual scholarship fee?

    The amount of the annual online scholarship paid by the sponsor is £1,200 per year, as half is paid by the private educational institution that takes the student to study. The total tuition fee is £2,400. We offer a private sponsor to pay 50% of the selected scholarship, as the project is implemented in partnership with Ukrainian private schools, that contribute to children's education. The offline scholarship for studying at school in Kyiv, which includes educational materials, stationery, meals, excursions, etc., is £3,600 per year, which is paid by the sponsor, the other half is paid by the school, totaling £7,200. And it makes it possible for a Ukrainian child to receive a full-fledged education, to receive food and outside classes in Kyiv, etc

    How exactly will my donation be used?

    All funds provided by the sponsor are used to cover half of the cost of the student's education in a private educational institution. Half is paid by the school and reports are provided.

    Can I cover the whole (2–3 year) scholarship fee for one child?

    Yes, you can close the need of 1 or more children, for any period. This scholarship will be attached to the student and you will be able to follow his progress.

    When do I have to donate to let the student start studying on time?

    Our organization has a permanent database of students who need a scholarship to study in a private institution. As soon as we receive funds for a scholarship, we immediately open new opportunities for new students.

    What will happen if I stop donating?

    If you do not have the opportunity to continue sponsoring a Ukrainian student's scholarship, he will automatically continue his studies at a private institution and we will look for a new sponsor for him. We have a lot of experience in providing scholarships and will continue, although the war has made its adjustments and we are forced to peck like-minded people.

    How can I find out about the student's achievements and problems during the school year?

    Our foundation will provide a semester report on the success of the student's victory. If you wish, you can also join our free "support club" and have constant contact with the student and his family. Thus, to know not only about his academic success, but also about development in general.

    How can I keep in touch with the student and family I helped?

    The goal of our foundation is to create new connections between Ukrainians and people around the world for the sake of supporting children. For us, not only financial support is very important, but also moral and emotional support - that's why we created a "support club" where all our sponsors can communicate with schools, teachers, and families participating in the program.
